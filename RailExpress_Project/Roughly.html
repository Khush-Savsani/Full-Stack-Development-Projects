About Us

Our Legacy

Serving millions of passengers since 1853, we're India's largest and most trusted railway network, connecting people and places across the nation.

Our Mission

To provide safe, efficient, and comfortable travel experiences while continuously innovating and modernizing our services for better customer satisfaction.

Our Commitment

We're committed to excellence in service, passenger safety, and environmental sustainability, making your journey comfortable and memorable.

Our railway network spans over 67,000 kilometers, making it one of the largest in the world. We connect more than 7,300 stations across India, operating over 20,000 passenger trains daily and serving billions of passengers annually.

We offer various classes of travel to suit every budget and preference, from luxurious first-class AC coaches to economical general class. Our services include e-ticketing, food delivery, wheelchair assistance, and real-time train tracking.

Passenger safety is our top priority. We implement rigorous safety protocols, regular track maintenance, and modern signaling systems. Our dedicated Railway Protection Force ensures security across all stations and trains.

We're committed to reducing our environmental impact through solar power initiatives, bio-toilets in trains, water recycling systems, and increasing electrification of our network to reduce carbon emissions.
