Frequently Asked Questions

To book a train ticket, start by visiting our home page where you will be prompted to enter your journey details such as the source, destination, travel date, and travel class. Once you submit the form, our system will display a list of available trains that match your criteria.

Select your preferred train and proceed to choose your seats. You will then be required to enter your personal details and make a payment through one of our secure payment options (UPI, card, or net banking). Once the transaction is successfully completed, an electronic ticket (E-ticket) is generated instantly.

Our interface is designed to guide you through each step with clear instructions, ensuring that even first-time users can complete the booking process with ease.

We offer a variety of secure payment options to suit your preferences. These include:

  • UPI: A quick and secure method using popular UPI apps.
  • Debit/Credit Cards: With validation to ensure correct card details.

Each payment method undergoes strict security checks to protect your personal and financial information. Our system confirms your payment in real time and sends you an electronic ticket.

Once your ticket is booked, you will be provided with a 10-digit PNR number. You can check your booking status by navigating to our dedicated PNR status page and entering your PNR number.

The system then retrieves your booking details, including train timings, seat allocation, travel date, and other relevant information. This service is available 24/7 and provides real-time updates on your journey.

If you need to cancel your ticket, go to our Ticket Cancellation page and enter your 10-digit PNR number. The cancellation process is guided by a two-step confirmation to ensure that you do not cancel your ticket accidentally.

Once you confirm your cancellation, your refund is processed according to our cancellation policy. Please note that cancellation charges may apply, and the refund amount is calculated based on the fare and the time of cancellation.

Our customer support team is available around the clock to assist you with any issues you may encounter during the booking process. Whether you face technical difficulties, payment issues, or require general assistance, you can contact us via our helpline or email.

Additionally, our website provides a comprehensive FAQ section (this page) that covers common queries related to train ticket booking, payment, PNR status, and cancellations. We continuously improve our services based on customer feedback to ensure a smooth and efficient booking experience.
